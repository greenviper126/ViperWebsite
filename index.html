01 About Me who I am

I'm a self-taught programmer with 5+ years of experience, specializing in systems and gameplay programming. My main platform is Roblox, though I also have experience shipping small games in Unity.

I enjoy building modular systems that support gameplay and make development more maintainable. My work focuses on both gameplay and backend tooling in Luau — frameworks, NPC AI, procedural generation, and the atmospheric systems that make horror games work.
