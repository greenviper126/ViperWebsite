About Me

I am a self-taught systems and gameplay programmer with 5+ years of hands-on experience building interactive projects. My primary development platform is Roblox (Luau), with additional shipped work in Unity (C#).

I focus on modular architecture, gameplay systems, and internal tooling that improve long-term maintainability. My work includes framework design, NPC behavior systems, procedural generation, and atmospheric mechanics for horror-focused player experiences.
